SouqNote Privacy Policy

Last updated: Dec 3, 2025

Thank you for using SouqNote (“we”, “our”, or “us”). Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

SouqNote does not collect or store any personal data on company-owned servers. All business records, sales, and related data you enter into the app remain on your device and are synchronized only to your personal Google Drive account under a secure AppData folder.

We do not have access to this data and cannot view, modify, or share it.

The only information accessed temporarily is:

  • Your Google account ID (to connect to your Drive for backup and sync)
  • App-related data stored in your Google Drive AppData folder

This process is managed entirely by Google’s API and is never shared with us or third parties.

2. How Your Data Is Used

SouqNote uses your data solely to:

  • Store and synchronize your business records securely to your Google Drive
  • Restore your data when reinstalling or switching devices
  • Maintain consistent app performance and reliability

We do not sell, analyze, or transfer your data to any other entity.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is saved locally on your device and in your Google Drive AppData folder.

We do not operate any external servers or databases.

Synchronization is encrypted and handled through Google’s secure API systems.

You can delete SouqNote data from your Google Drive at any time.

4. Third-Party Access

SouqNote integrates the Google Drive API solely for backup and restore. This connection is authorized by you through Google’s sign-in process and is bound by:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google API Services User Data Policy

SouqNote does not share your Google account information with any third party.

5. Children’s Privacy

SouqNote is intended for business and professional use. We do not knowingly collect or process data from children under 13 years of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
